SEVENTEEN (세븐틴)

SEVENTEEN is a self-producing idol group known for their sharp choreography, vocals, and genuine teamwork. Their passion shines through every performance. 💎

I love their energy, their bond as a group, and how they always try new styles while staying true to themselves. They always lift my mood!

🎧 Song I recommend: "Circles" — a touching and emotional ballad that captures feelings of hope and healing.
